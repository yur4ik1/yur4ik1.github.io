Financial Institutions

Our suite offers international data coverage, including global and local sanction lists such as

1. Global and local sanction lists: Our software provides access to global and local sanction lists, including those published by the United Nations (UN) and the Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) in the United States. Sanctions are government-imposed trade restrictions on countries, organizations, or individuals that pose a risk to national security or international peace.

2. PEP and RCA: Our software provides PEP (Politically Exposed Person) and RCA (Related Corporate Authority) data for more than 200 countries. PEPs are individuals who hold prominent public positions, such as politicians or government officials, and are considered high-risk for financial crime. RCAs are individuals who have control over or are closely related to a company that is deemed high-risk.

3. International PEP and RCAs: Our software provides international PEP and RCA data from organizations such as the UN and NATO, adding an extra layer of protection against financial crime.

4. Global export controls: Our software provides data on global export controls, which are government-imposed restrictions on the export of certain goods or technology that could be used for military purposes or contribute to the development of weapons of mass destruction.

5. Adverse Media: Our software provides access to adverse media, which includes news articles, social media posts, and other publicly available information that could indicate a risk of financial crime.

6. Divers data: Our software offers diverse data such as "Global Most Wanted," tax evasion, corruption, and regulatory enforcement to help identify potential risks beyond the usual PEP and sanction lists.
