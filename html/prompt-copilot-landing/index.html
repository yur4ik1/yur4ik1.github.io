    FAQ

    What is AI prompt enhancement and how does it work?

    AI prompt enhancement automatically improves your ChatGPT prompts by adding structure, clarity, and context. Our prompt optimization tool analyzes your input and suggests improvements for better AI responses, making prompt engineering effortless for both beginners and advanced users.

    How does AI prompt autocomplete save time?

    Prompt autocomplete predicts and completes your prompts as you type, similar to Gmail's Smart Compose. This AI writing assistant feature reduces typing time by up to 70% and helps overcome writer's block when crafting complex prompts.

    What are AI context profiles and why do I need them?

    Context profiles store your background information, preferences, and frequently used context for AI conversations. Instead of re-explaining your job role, project details, or personal context every session, our context management system automatically applies relevant information to your prompts.

    Does Prompt Copilot work with ChatGPT and other AI tools?

    Absolutely! Prompt Copilot works seamlessly with ChatGPT and we're actively adding support for Claude, Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and Grok interfaces. Our enhanced prompts improve AI response quality across all major language models.

    Are my context profiles secure and private?

    Yes! Context profiles are encrypted and stored securely. We never access your personal information and you maintain full control over your data with options to export, edit, or delete profiles at any time.

    Is Prompt Copilot compatible with my browser?

    Our Chrome extension works with all Chromium-based browsers including Chrome, Edge, and Brave. We're developing versions for Firefox and Safari to expand AI prompt tool accessibility across all platforms.
