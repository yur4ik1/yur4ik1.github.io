Welcome!

ENTRECOMP4TRANSITION together with NINJABLE are presenting you with the tool that will allow you to reflect on your experience and confidence to identify your strengths and areas you would like to improve.

You will have had plenty of experiences across different areas of your life - leisure, family, community, education or work. As a result of these different experiences, how experienced and confident do you feel in your ability to think or act entrepreneurially?

We have prepared a set of 15 scenarios and after completing them all you will be guided through your identified competence areas and skills. Answer each question by selecting the option which best describes your approach.

Completing the tool can be a standalone activity or used to motivate yourself before and after undertaking any training. You could also use it as your personal tracking diary to understand what skills you need to develop and how.
